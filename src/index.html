About Us

Lucky Coffee, founded by Jiahui, is a unique café where customers input their daily lucky number to receive personalized coffee recommendations. This innovative blend of coffee and luck turned the café into a bustling social hub. Skilled baristas crafted bespoke brews, making each visit an exciting experience. Beyond the aromatic blends, Lucky Coffee became a space where friendships flourished and creativity sparked. The café's success lies in transforming ordinary coffee breaks into moments of serendipity, reminding patrons that the best experiences combine passion, innovation, and a touch of cosmic magic.

Arabica coffee beans,

originating from Ethiopia, are celebrated for their smooth, mild flavor and balanced acidity. Often considered the connoisseur's choice, these beans boast a nuanced taste profile, featuring hints of sweetness and subtle notes of fruit, making them a favorite among coffee lovers worldwide.

Robusta coffee beans,

predominantly grown in Africa and Southeast Asia, are known for their robust, strong flavor and higher caffeine content compared to Arabica beans. With a slightly bitter taste and earthy notes, Robusta beans are commonly used in espresso blends, adding depth and a lingering, bold finish to the brew.

Liberica coffee beans,

originating from West Africa, have a distinct flavor profile characterized by woody and smoky notes. Unlike the more common Arabica and Robusta beans, Liberica beans have an irregular shape and a unique taste that appeals to those seeking a different coffee experience.
