About Us

Lucky Coffee, founded by Jiahui, is a unique café where customers input their daily lucky number to receive personalized coffee recommendations. This innovative blend of coffee and luck turned the café into a bustling social hub. Skilled baristas crafted bespoke brews, making each visit an exciting experience. Beyond the aromatic blends, Lucky Coffee became a space where friendships flourished and creativity sparked. The café's success lies in transforming ordinary coffee breaks into moments of serendipity, reminding patrons that the best experiences combine passion, innovation, and a touch of cosmic magic.
